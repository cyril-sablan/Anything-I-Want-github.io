About Asahi Azumane

Asahi Azumane is a third year in Karasuno High and is Karasunos #3 playing as the wing spiker and holding the title of the current ace.

Despite his appearance Asahi is rather quiet and gentle and can be doubtful towards his ability of being an ace. Asahi use to be take everything said about him to heart and would often be depressed and affect his performance on the court.

Admired and loved by his team many aspire to be like Asahi as he shows an incredible feet of strength and later started to overcome what others said about him realizing his abilities are greater than he thinks.

His strength is evident in his power and ability displayed through his spikes and killer serves being able to break through many powerful teams defences.
