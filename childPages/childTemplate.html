About Shōyō Hinata

Shōyō Hinata is a first-year student attending Karasuno High and Karasunos #10 middle blocker.

Ever since middle school until his arrival to Karasuno, he was always underestimated by his lack of height being stood at 162.8cm.

However, what separates him from the rest is his incredible feet of speed and jumping abilities. His intense desire to strive to improve at volleyball drives his impressive tenacity for wanting to play and win at volleyball.
