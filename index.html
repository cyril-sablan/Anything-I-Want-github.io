Karasuno Highschool is located in Miyagi Prefecture of Northern Japan, The mascot of the school is associated with crows due to the banner the school has which reads "fly" which alludes to crows.

Karasuno represented Miyagi prefecture at the Spring High nationals. However, after the initial success Karasuno had started to fail in their attempts to reach Nationals which gave them the derogatory nickname "The Fallen Champions" or "The Flightless Crows."

Karasuno in regards to their volleyball team were once considered a powerhouse school but have fallen for years up until the present year of which this show is based on.
